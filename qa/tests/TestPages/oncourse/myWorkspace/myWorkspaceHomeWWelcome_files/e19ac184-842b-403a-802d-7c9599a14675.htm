    Give feedback to Oncourse site participants

    Instructors and site leaders: Using the Oncourse PostEm tool, you can upload a spreadsheet to present feedback to site participants. You can calculate and display simple statistics for just about any numerical data. See Oncourse PostEm tool: Overview. Watch the feature demos. [Posted 4/4/13]

    Share your Oncourse wiki pages

    Anyone at IU can add the Wiki tool to Oncourse My Workspace and share pages with others. Site owners can also share from a course or project site. Watch the feature demos. See Oncourse Wiki: Overview. To share a wiki page with anyone, see Using Oncourse, how do I set permissions for a wiki page?. [Posted 4/2/13]

    Publish a podcast using Oncourse

    Instructors and site leaders: With the Podcasts tool, you can store and distribute multimedia content to site participants that they can download manually, through RSS, or using software such as iTunes. Content can take different forms, including audio, video, and slideshows. See Oncourse Podcasts. Watch the feature demos. [Posted 3/28/13]
