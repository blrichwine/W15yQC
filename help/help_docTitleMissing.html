Help for "Failure: Document Title is empty." (code: docTitleMissing)

Background

Having a descriptive and accurate title allows people to choose your page above others in a list of search engine results. The title of a web page is displayed in search engine results. The title is displayed at the top left of your web browser. The title is also displayed as "tabs" in web browsers.

W15yQC Checks

Example Failures

QA Pages

  • Document Titles Missing QA Page: qa_docTitleMissing.html

Suggestions

Titles should be page specific. If you had one chance to tell people what that one specific web page is about, what would you say? Add a TITLE element as a child of the HEAD element: 

    <html>
      <head>
        <title>Downloads - W15yQC</title>
      </head>
      <body>
		...
If each page in your website has the same title, you are not following this guideline. If any two pages in your website has the same title, you are not following this guideline. If you title does not accurately describe the contents of the page you are not following this guideline. If the title of your "dog" page on your site is "Dog dogs dogsdogs dawgz dog dog doggy dog" you are not following this guideline.

Related WCAG 2.0 Success Criteria

  • WCAG 2.0 SC 2.4.2 Page Titled: Web pages have titles that describe topic or purpose. (Level A) (understanding 2.4.2) (how to meet 2.4.2)

Related Section 508 Standards

  • §1194.22 (i) - Frames shall be titled with text that facilitates frame identification and navigation.
